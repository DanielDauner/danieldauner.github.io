Daniel Dauner


I am a master's student in computer science at the University of Tübingen. I am excited about deep learning, computer vision, and robotics. I will finish my master's thesis by August 2023 and I am actively searching for phd positions!

Bio: I did a bachelor's degree in bioinformatics at the University of Tübingen. Following that, I started my master's in computer science at the University of Tübingen. I am writing my thesis about the nuPlan challenge in the Autonomous Vision Group, led by Prof. Andreas Geiger.

Awards: At the chair of Prof. Andreas Zell, I won the Diana Chess AI competition in 2020 and the Deep Learning competition in 2021 (code). In the winter term 2021/2022, I competed in the Self-Driving Cars lecture by Prof. Andreas Geiger, where I won all three challenges about imitation learning, reinforcement learning, and modular pipeline agents. Recently, our team won the 2023 nuPlan planning challenge!
